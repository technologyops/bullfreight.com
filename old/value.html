VALUES


Integrity:

A deal is only a good deal if all parties find it attractive – otherwise it should never have been made.
Customers assigning tasks to us will rest assure that we never provide a job not done…
Integrity is also about mutual trust. Trust in the fact that we always find the right price every time and trust in the fact that we always come through on tasks given to us.

Selectivity:

We work with partners and suppliers who have the same high standards to quality and service as we do.

In a constant search for the ultimate mix of suppliers and partners, we spend much time on dialogue with suppliers – both current and especially potential suppliers. Thus we is ensures that we always provide the right concepts to our customers.

Selectivity is also about knowing our limitations and we cannot and will not be something for everybody – we will however be everything to somebody.

 Creativity:

If we are to survive in the battle against the global players we must be able to provide ultimate creativity in the solutions we present to our customers. This is a fact that demands from us that we know what we are doing and thus our employees must be among the best in the industry.

Finding the right solution is not always about finding the cheapest price. Our task is to provide alternatives to our customers. These alternatives must be presented and discussed with our customers enabling us as partners to choose the right solutions – which will be the right choice backed by economy pricing.

Flexibility:
The world keeps changing and our customers are constantly presented with new challenges. Changes in sourcing patterns, customers and the continuous globalization, constantly affect our customers everyday life.

These demands are of course being pushed on to us as a logistics provider and we must keep up pace. With very short line of command we can and will react with very short notice if needed thus enabling us to provide ultimate flexibility.

As another sign of flexibility we have 24/7 service on our phones enabling us to communicate with partners globally and also giving us the possibility of monitoring time crucial shipments by following them very closely.
