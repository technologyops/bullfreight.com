About the company


The trend in the logistics industry has all along been -“ bigger is better” - but after having tried the global systems ourselves we believe it is time to present an alternative way to create a Customer focused company .

Bull Freight Global Solutions believe in creating truly competitive concepts within all transport forms through aggressive buying politics, skilled and experienced staff with the ability to create creative solutions, but especially by focusing all our efforts in providing ultimate and superior customer service to customers who today may lack this from some of the global logistics providers.

We wish to (re)implement the concept of a house forwarder where no tasks or jobs are too small or too cumbersome. Our value lies in linking strong suppliers with the right customers and adding ultimate customer service and follow-up to each and every job we do.

It is also our hope and ambition that the many years we have spent in the industry will enable us to act as a consultant and an extra resource to our customers in a continuous search to improve and fine tune our customers existing logistical concepts.

After more than 25 years in the industry, the last 6 years with A.P. Moeller-MAERSK , it is time to put the lifelong dream of creating an own company to the ultimate test. Please see more about our values and solutions elsewhere on the site and please do not hesitate in contacting me for additional comments or questions.  

We wish to prove that there are alternatives and our voice to the customer will always be:
 
     We are not something for everybody – but we are everything to somebody
