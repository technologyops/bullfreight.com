Products and Services


Airfreight:

All our staff has airfreight background also and we have our own group company IATA licensed which we allow us to discuss rates and services directly with carriers in all major Locations Airports in India . This ensures that we keep developing our skills and contacts in an ever changing market. we have good contacts with Carriers for many years and can present interesting alternatives on especially the larger spot shipments.

Understanding the full cost picture we realize that destination costs often is a considerable part of the total airfreight costs. Therefore we often present an alternative door/door price using our very contacts with the leading overseas partners enabling us to give total costs and transit time at the time of request from our customers.

With this mix of alternatives we are able to assist with airfreight shipments from the smallest minimum shipment to full charter aircrafts.

Ocean freight:

We cooperate with both with shipping lines/agents but also with trusted partners in the freight forwarding market. By this we can identify the best solutions with the quality we and our customers expect.

We try whenever possible to provide our customers with more than one option on actual requests and we openly discuss the various benefits and challenges of these solutions with our customers.

We take full responsibility in terms of the dialogue with shipping lines including booking, pricing , documentation, invoicing and follow-up.

We always try to present more than one solution to the customer ensuring that we not just focus on cheapest price but also take other factors into consideration.

Customs handling:

We have our own customs transacting License which enable us to handle origin customs clearances and Door Delivery of shipments at all major Ports/Airports in India.
